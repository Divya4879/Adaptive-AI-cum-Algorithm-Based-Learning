Modified Leitner System Algorithm

Our adaptive learning platform uses a Modified Leitner System for spaced repetition combined with dynamic level progression. Questions you answer incorrectly are added to your practice pool, where they must be answered correctly 5 times to be considered mastered. The algorithm optimizes learning efficiency by focusing on your weak areas while maintaining knowledge retention.
